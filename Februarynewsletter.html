Changing your cartridge filter yearly is an important part of pool maintenance. Filters are one of the most important pieces of pool equipment. The filter is responsible for keeping your pool healthy, clean, and clear. The pool filter is responsible for straining out dirt, debris, oil, suntan lotion, etc. Over time these pool filter cartridges get clogged up with dirt, debris, algae, and other contaminant particles. They also can wear down, tear, rupture, and break. To ensure these cartridges are effective, they need to be cleaned and replaced periodically. We will email you to advise when your filter needs to be changed.
Poolside Recipe
Agave Kiss
Surprise your Valentine with a delicious drink by the pool. This sweet, creamy cocktail is sure to make them swoon.
INGREDIENTS:
  • 2 ounces Tequila
  • 1 ounce white creme de cacao
  • 1 ounce heavy cream
  • 1/2 ounce Chambord
  • White chocolate flakes for rim
  • Fresh raspberries for garnish
DIRECTIONS:
Pour the ingredients into a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake for 15 seconds. Pour into a chilled cocktail glass rimmed with white chocolate, add a spear of fresh raspberries, and serve.
